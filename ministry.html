Music is my ministry. Every note is a prayer.

Where Music Meets Ministry

For Kirk Whalum, music has always been more than entertainment. It's a calling. With a Masters of Art in Religion and ordination as a minister, Kirk bridges the worlds of jazz and faith in a way few artists can.

Whether he's leading worship at a church, teaching students at Visible Music College, or recording in the studio, the same spiritual intention flows through everything Kirk touches. His ministry isn't separate from his music — it IS his music.

Through the Couragement Campaign, Kirk is on a mission to inspire daily courage through faith, music, and community.

What is Couragement?

Couragement is Kirk's word for the courage that comes from encouragement. It's a daily practice of lifting others up, finding strength in faith, and using music as a vehicle for transformation. The campaign brings together Kirk's audience through daily inspiration, exclusive content, and a shared mission to spread light.
